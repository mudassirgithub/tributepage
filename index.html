DAVID J MALAN

Gordon McKay Professor of the Practice of Computer Science at Harvard University
Malan teaching the cs50 course

    Here's the timeline of David J Malan's Life

  • 1995-1999 : Bachelor of Arts at Harvard University
  • 1999-2000 : Maths Teacher at Franklin High School
  • 2000-2001 : Engineering Manager at AirClic Inc.
  • 2002-2004 : Master of Science at Harvard University
  • 2002-2005 : Instructor for Computer Science at Tufts University
  • 2005-2005 : Assisted police and prosecutors with criminal investigations at Office of the Middlesex District Attorney, Special Investigations Division
  • 2002-2007 : Ph.D in Computer Science at Harvard University
  • 2005-2008 : Started his company named Diskaster
  • 2008-2011 : Chief Information Officer (CIO) at Mindset Media
  • 2003-2012 : Massachusetts-licensed Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)
  • 2007-present: Senior member of the Faculty of Arts and Sciences. Instructor for CS50
